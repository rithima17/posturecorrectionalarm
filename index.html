Welcome to Posture Correction Alarm

This project is mainly designed for the people who are doing Work at Home(WFH). We used to sit infront of the Computer for long times neglecting the Posture. But this leads to Severe Back Pain. By Switching on, using the help of TensorFlow.js, It automatically detects the Posture if you move left or right from the Screen and will notify you with a Warning Sound, and it Blurs the Screen.

Features:
  • Real-time AI posture tracking
  • Automated alert system
  • Computer vision-based analysis
  • Prevents long-term spinal issues
Instructions to Use:
  • Click the Start Button Below to start Capturing
  • Now the System recongnises the position of Eyes, and keeps it as Default.
  • Now, Move right or Left from the initial direction before
  • You will notice a Warning Sound and the Screen gets Blurred.
  • Until you sit in a Right Posture i.e, Initial Posture the Warning Sound will not terminate.
